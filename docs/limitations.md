# Limitations

- Citation Check is not a general fact-checking system.
- It does not access paid academic databases.
- It cannot verify claims against sources that are not provided or publicly accessible.
- TF-IDF/BM25 retrieval can find related text but cannot fully understand complex scholarly entailment.
- DOI metadata can confirm that a work exists, but not whether the full paper supports the claim.
- Author-year and bibliography search can return plausible but wrong candidates, so non-high-confidence matches are shown for review instead of being attached automatically.
- Metadata-only matches cannot verify direct quotes or claim support without parsed full text.
- Direct quote verification depends on the quality of extracted PDF text.
- Page numbers may be affected by PDF front matter or extraction quirks.
